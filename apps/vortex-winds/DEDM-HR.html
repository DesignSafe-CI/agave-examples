VORTEX-Winds: Database-Enabled Design Module for High-Rise (DEDM-HR). Navigate to the web application developed by the NatHaz Modeling Laboratory, Univ. of Notre Dame which seamlessly pools databases of multiple high frequency base balance measurements through wind tunnel tests/experiments from geographically dispersed locations and merges them together to expand the number of available building configurations for preliminary design under winds.
